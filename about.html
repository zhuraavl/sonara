About our Hotel and Design

The Sonora Inn features a wonderful variety of historic Sonora, CA hotel rooms and suites. With seven different room types, we can accommodate any guest or group. Enjoying a night alone with your loved one? Explore our spacious King suite. Traveling with family? Our two-double or two-queen bed guest rooms can host your entire crew! We can also accommodate tour groups or wedding parties for those enjoying a special visit to the beautiful Sonora, CA and Yosemite area.
